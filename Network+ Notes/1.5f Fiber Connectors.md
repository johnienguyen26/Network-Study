# Fiber Connectors 1.5f
## Subscriber Connector (SC)

<img src="images/subcriber connector.png" width="200" align="right"> 

- Not actually an abbreviation
  - Square Connector
  - Standard Connector
- Pushes on to lock
  - Pull connectory to unlock
- A popular fiber connector
  - Common in many data centers
## Local Connector (LC)

<img src="images/local connector.png" width="300" align="right">

- Another popular fiber type
  - Smaller and more compact connector
- Locks in place with a clip
  - Press to release
- Other names
  - Lucent Connector
  - Little Connector
## Straight Tip (ST)

<img src="images/straight tip.png" width="275" align="right">


- Bayonet Connector
  - Stick and twist
- Push on and turn
  - Locks in place
  - Turn to unlock
## Multi-fiber Push on

<img src="images/MPO connector.png" width="275" align="right">

- Twelve fibers in a single connector
  - Save space and manage one cable
- Push to lock in place
  - Pull connector to unlock
- May also see the MTP abbreviation
  - A corning brand
  - The MTP MPO connector
